Here’s what you need to do in this code challenge:

You should write 6 JPQL queries to retrieve this data.
1. A list of all the students
2. A list of students who are attending classes on Monday
3. Average rating for the teacher named “White”
4. Average ratings for teachers (Hint: use the GROUP BY clause)
5. Average ratings for teachers arranged in the descending order of rating (Hint: use the ORDER BY clause)
6. Average ratings for teachers having an average rating greater than 3, arranged in the descending order of rating (Hint: use the HAVING clause)
